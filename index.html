    Preparation

    At JAM Plastering, we understand that proper preparation is the foundation of a successful plastering project. Our skilled team ensures that surfaces are carefully inspected, cleaned, and any necessary repairs are made before applying plaster. By taking these essential steps, we guarantee a smooth and even surface, ensuring the best results for your project.

    Skimming

    Skimming is a technique used to create a flawless surface on interior walls and ceilings. By applying a thin layer of plaster, we can conceal imperfections, cracks, and uneven textures, resulting in a polished and professional appearance. Our expert skimming services offer a quick and cost-effective solution for achieving a refined and attractive finish.

    Custom Finishes

    Looking to add a unique touch to your interiors? Our custom plaster finishes are the perfect solution. Whether you desire a specific texture, pattern, or colour, our experienced craftsmen can create tailor-made finishes to match your vision. From intricate designs to modern textures, we provide a wide range of options to elevate the aesthetics of your space. Let us bring your ideas to life with our exceptional custom plaster finishes.

    Rendering

    Rendering is a crucial process in construction, where a durable, weather-resistant coating is applied to exterior walls. It enhances the aesthetics and protects the building from elements like rain, wind, and UV rays. Our skilled plastering team ensures a smooth and seamless finish, using high-quality materials to guarantee long-lasting results.

    Dry Lining

    Dry lining is an efficient way to transform interior spaces. It involves attaching plasterboard directly to walls, providing a smooth base for further decoration. This technique reduces drying time, making it an ideal choice for time-sensitive projects. Our skilled team ensures precision installation and exceptional finishing, giving your walls a modern and stylish look.

    Venetian Plastering

    Venetian plastering, an ancient technique, adds elegance and sophistication to any space. Using multiple layers of fine plaster, we create a luxurious, marble-like effect that catches and reflects light beautifully. Our artisans masterfully apply Venetian plaster, resulting in a stunning, bespoke finish that brings a touch of timeless artistry to your interiors.
